Fraud Order Identification¶

We use extension ‘Signifyd_Connect’ to identify the order is fraud one or not. And we make some changs during integration to make it suit Toolots, here’s the things need to pay attention to

  1. Just orders paid with braintree credit card are allowed to send to signifyd

  2. To make signifyd identify ‘Will Call’ order , we made some change followed by signifyd’s guidance

  3. Invoice generation should be triggered after that one order are approved by signifyd
